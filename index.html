Option: Free

Great for small businesses

With the free version of our membership plan you get, the full functionally of the app but with limitations. These limitations include: In app ads, 5GB of cloud storage a month, no 24/7 customer support from our team members and only 1 (one) admin account.

Option: Monthly
Great for any businesses

With the monthly version of our membership plan you get the full functionally of the app, this includes no in app adds, 24/7 support from out team members and the ability to have mutliple admin accounts. The only difference between this and the yearly subscription is you only get 15 GB of cloud storage a month.

Option: Yearly
Great for large businesses

With the yearly version of our membership plan you get, the full functionally with zero limitations. This means you get unlimited monthly cloud storage, 24/7 customer support form our team members, unlimited admin accounts and no in app ads.
