About

When we created this device, we had one thing in mind, Safety. With the specific sensors we used, we were able to create a device that needs no contact to work. The sensors we used are: temperature sensor, ultrasonic sensor, motion sensor and lastly a motor driver. Look below to see what each sensor does.

The MLX90614 is an infrared thermometer for non-contact temperature measurements. It has a temperature in range of -20 to 120Â°C. In this project it is used to check the usesrs temperature before enterting the building

An ultrasonic sensor is an electronic device that measures the distance of a target object by emitting ultrasonic sound waves, and converts the reflected sound into an electrical signal. For our project, it is used to indicate to whether the user is in a reasonable range for the temperature sensor to get an accurate temperature.

A motion detector is an electrical device that utilizes a sensor to detect nearby motion. It is used to turn on the whole system and let all the other sensors know they are about to be used.

The TB6612FNG is capable of driving two motors at up to 1.2A of constant current. Once the temperature sensors detects a temperature thats in a reasonable range, it will send a signal to the motor driver to move the lock allowing the person enter the building.
